# Differentiators

## Not just a complaint form

Most solutions stop at "submit complaint." CivicPulse AI converts reports into:
- severity,
- category,
- responsible team,
- recommended action,
- duplicate key,
- public status,
- admin workflow.

## Not just a chatbot

Gemini is used for structured operations data, not generic chat.

## Not fake government tech

The app is honest:
- it does not claim official integration,
- it says reports are added to the community dashboard,
- it has a path for future official integration.

## Portfolio value
