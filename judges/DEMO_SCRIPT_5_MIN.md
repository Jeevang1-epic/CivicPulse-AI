# 5-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00–0:30 — Hook

"Most local problems are not ignored because people don't care. They are ignored because reports are scattered, duplicated, and hard to prioritize. CivicPulse AI solves that by turning local complaints into structured, trackable action."

## 0:30–1:00 — Problem and track fit

"We selected Community Hero - Hyperlocal Problem Solver. Our focus is neighborhoods, campuses, and local communities where people need a simple way to report problems and a transparent way to see progress."

## 1:00–1:50 — Citizen report flow

"Here a citizen reports an issue. I will enter: water leakage on the main road is making the road slippery for bikes. The user does not need to know the exact department or severity. They just describe what they saw."

Submit report.

## 1:50–2:40 — AI triage

"Gemini now converts this messy report into structured civic data: title, category, severity, safety level, duplicate key, responsible team, recommended action, and a citizen-friendly response. This is the important part — AI is not just chatting, it is powering workflow."

## 2:40–3:20 — Public board

"Now the report appears on the public board. Other people can support the issue instead of creating duplicates. The status is visible, so the community does not feel like reports disappear."

## 3:20–4:10 — Admin dashboard

"On the dashboard, admins and volunteers can see urgent issues first, filter by category, and update status from open to in-review, assigned, or resolved. The AI action plan helps the team decide the next step quickly."

## 4:10–4:40 — Google Cloud and architecture

"The app is deployed on Google Cloud Run. Firestore stores reports and live status, and Gemini handles structured triage through a secure server-side API route, so the key is never exposed in the browser."

## 4:40–5:00 — Closing

"CivicPulse AI can scale from a college campus to a residential colony or ward-level community. It is a practical community hero system: report, triage, prioritize, act, and track."
