# One-liner Pitch

**CivicPulse AI turns messy local complaints into prioritized, trackable community action using Gemini, Firestore, and Google Cloud Run.**

## 30-second pitch

In every neighborhood, problems are reported in scattered WhatsApp groups, calls, and conversations, but they rarely become structured action. CivicPulse AI lets citizens report local issues, uses Gemini to classify urgency and suggest next steps, and gives volunteers/admins a live dashboard to prioritize and resolve what matters first.

## 60-second pitch

CivicPulse AI is a hyperlocal civic issue-response system. A citizen can report a problem like a streetlight outage, garbage pile, water leak, or unsafe road. Gemini transforms that report into a structured issue with category, severity, duplicate key, responsible team, and recommended action. Firestore keeps the board live, and the Cloud Run deployed dashboard lets admins or volunteers update status and track progress. The result is not just reporting — it is a transparent community workflow from problem to resolution.
